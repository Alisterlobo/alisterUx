About Me

Crafting visuals that leave a lasting impression

Hi, I'm Alister — a passionate graphic designer based in Mumbai, India. I specialise in creating bold, meaningful visual identities that communicate the right message to the right audience.

With expertise across Adobe Photoshop, Canva, Affinity Designer, and Adobe Express, I bring ideas to life through thoughtful design — whether it's a stunning flyer, a scroll-stopping social media post, or a complete brand visual.

I believe great design isn't just about aesthetics — it's about telling a story, building connection, and driving results.
